About Xtreme Fitness Gym

Xtreme Fitness Gym is designed for people who take their training seriously. Heavy weights, real effort, and a focused environment where you come to work – not to waste time.

From strength and hypertrophy to conditioning and martial arts, Xtreme Fitness Gym will be a complete facility in Western Sydney for those who want real results.

Equipment & Services

A serious setup for serious training.

Strength Area

Plate-loaded and selectorised machines, squat racks, benches, Olympic bars and heavy dumbbells – built for progressive overload.

Free Weights & Platforms

Custom dumbbells and plates with Xtreme Fitness Gym branding, rubber flooring and platforms for squats, bench and deadlifts.

Cardio & Conditioning

Cardio machines to support fat loss, warm-ups and conditioning without taking over the gym floor.

Future Extras

Saunas, combat / Jiu-Jitsu area and coaching options are part of the long-term plan as the gym grows.
